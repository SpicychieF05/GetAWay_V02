Product Requirements Document (PRD)
GetAWay
Browser-Native AI-Proctored Interview Platform
Version: 2.0 (Rebuild)

1. Product Vision
GetAWay aims to become the most accessible and trustworthy browser-based interview platform for technical recruitment.
GetAWay emphasizes interview integrity more than traditional video conferencing tools by integrating multiple features.
Live video interviews
Browser-native participation
AI-assisted behavioral monitoring
Recruiter-focused trust insights
Post-session integrity reporting
The platform should allow recruiters to evaluate candidates with greater confidence while minimizing friction for candidates.

2. Problem Statement
Remote hiring has significantly increased across industries.
However, recruiters face multiple challenges:
Limited visibility during interviews
Candidate impersonation risks
External assistance during technical rounds
Tab switching and distraction
Lack of trustworthy integrity signals
Dependence on generic video conferencing platforms
Existing proctoring tools are often:
Expensive
Difficult to adopt
Privacy invasive
Installation heavy
GetAWay solves these issues through browser-native participation and lightweight AI-assisted monitoring.

7. AI Signal Definitions
The system should support the following signal types.
Face Missing
Trigger:
No face detected for X seconds.
Impact:
Trust score reduction.

Multiple Faces
Trigger:
More than one face visible.
Impact:
High-severity alert.

Tab Switch
Trigger:
Browser tab becomes inactive.
Impact:
Medium-severity alert.

Audio Anomaly
Trigger:
Unexpected secondary voice or unusual noise.
Impact:
Medium-severity alert.

Camera Disabled
Trigger:
Video stream interruption.
Impact:
High-severity alert.
